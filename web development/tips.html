Travel Tips

Plan Your Kelantan Trip

3 Days 2 Nights

Enjoy a complete experience by visiting Pasar Siti Khadijah, Pantai Cahaya Bulan and other attractions around Kelantan.

How To Get There

Travel by bus, airplane or private vehicle depending on your preference.

Must Buy Products

Buy local products such as fish crackers and traditional snacks.

Transportation Guide

Transport Estimated Time
Airplane 1 Hour
Bus 8 Hours
Car 7 Hours

Useful Travel Tips

  • Bring enough cash for small shops and markets
  • Try local foods such as Nasi Kerabu and Nasi Dagang
  • Visit attractions early in the morning
  • Wear comfortable clothing suitable for hot weather
  • Respect local people and traditions
